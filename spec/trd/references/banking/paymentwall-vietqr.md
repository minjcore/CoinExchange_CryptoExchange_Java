# VietQR

VietQR is a standardized QR code payment system developed by NAPAS in collaboration with the Vietnam Banks Association. It enables fast and secure bank-to-bank transfers by scanning QR codes through mobile banking apps. Supporting direct transfers between participating Vietnamese banks, VietQR is widely accepted by merchants nationwide and ideal for personal payments, business transactions, and online shopping.

* * *

## Payment flow

- Step 1: Choose VietQR

![Bank Transfer Vietnam select payment method](https://docs.paymentwall.com/textures/pic/payment-system/bank-transfer/bt-vietqr/vietqr-1.jpg)
