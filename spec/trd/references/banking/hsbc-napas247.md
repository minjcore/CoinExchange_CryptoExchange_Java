# Instant Transfer NAPAS 247




Real-time sending and receiving anytime, anywhere


## What is Instant transfer NAPAS 247?

You can instantly send/ receive funds between banks accounts participating in NAPAS anytime, anywhere. Transfer in just a few simple steps by scanning your payee's VietQR code using your HSBC Vietnam App.

## Features and benefits

-


Real-time sending and receiving funds (see applicable fees [herehere About applicable fee of instant transfer NAPAS 247 service](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/en-vn/help/rates-and-fees/))


-


Add new payees to transfer money and conveniently save your payees for quick setup in the next transfers


-


Simply transfer by tapping ‘Scan QR code’ to scan, or choose a QR code from your gallery



-


Share payment details with the payee easily


-


Easy and secure, transfer in just a few simple steps



## How to make an Instant transfer NAPAS 247

### How to add a new payee

![Login to HSBC Vietnam app](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-1-en-account-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 1:**

Login to HSBC Vietnam app

Tap **Pay and transfer** icon at the bottom

![Make a transfer to proceed](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-2-en-payments-hub-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 2:**

In the next screen Tap **Make a transfer** to proceed

![select the account to transfer from](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-3-en-from-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 3:**

Tap account to select the account to transfer from

![Add a new payee icon](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-4-en-to-my-payees-1-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 4:**

Tap **Your payees**– the list will include all of your saved domestic payees; or

Tap **Add a new payee** icon

![Select payee account type](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-5-en-payee-bank-details-payee-type-option-2-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 5:**

Tap **Select payee type** in **Payee type** field

![Save payee](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-6-en-payee-bank-details-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 6:**

Enter payee details

Tap **Save payee** button if you want to save the payee details.

### How to select payment method "Instant transfer NAPAS 247"

![Select in Payment method field and select Instant transfer NAPAS 247](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-7-en-payment-details-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 1:**

Tap **Select** from the option in **Transfer method** field and select **Instant transfer NAPAS 247**

Enter transfer details then tap **Continue** to proceed

![Verify the transaction by face ID/ finger print or enter 6 digits PIN](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-8-en-face-id-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 2:**

Verify the transaction by face ID/ finger print or enter 6 digits PIN

![Review the details of the transfer in the Review screen and confirm](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-9-en-review-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 3:**

Review the details of the transfer in the Review screen

Tap **Confirm** if all details are correct

![Direct to confirmation page to complete the transfer](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/content/dam/hsbc/hbvn/images/ways-to-bank/screenshots/7137-10-en-confirmation-1600x900.jpg/_jcr_content/renditions/cq5dam.web.1280.1280.jpeg)

**Step 4:**

Upon tapping Confirm on the transfer details, you will be directed to the **Confirmation** showing the details of the completed transfer.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I make an Instant Transfer to credit card?

No. Currently, Instant Transfer does not support for credit card repayment.

### What are the transfer limits for Transfer using QR code and Instant Transfer NAPAS 247?

The per-transaction limit via ‘Scan QR code’ feature and Instant Transfer NAPAS 247 to non-HSBC payees ranges from VND 2,000 to VND 499,999,999.

The daily limit is subject to your account settings. For transactions via QR code, it follows your current setting of non-registered payees limit.

Follow these instructions to [change your daily limit](https://www.hsbc.com.vn/en-vn/ways-to-bank/online/faq/)

### Can I make an Instant transfer on Online Banking browser?

No. Currently, Instant transfer NAPAS 247 is only available on Mobile app.
