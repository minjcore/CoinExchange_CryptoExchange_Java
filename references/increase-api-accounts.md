Accounts

Copy for LLM

Accounts are your bank accounts with Increase. They store money, receive transfers, and send payments. They earn interest and have depository insurance.

Events

Your application can listen to [webhooks](https://increase.com/documentation/api/events) about this resource.The events about Accounts will have the categories"account.created"or"account.updated".
